Spider Conference 2019
======================

The task for this year's TCP (2019) was to create an online webshop for the F-guild. Necessary preparations should be done in the backend to fetch new products and display them in the app where it also should be possible to purchase the product. The store should consist of atleast 5 products with name, price and a picture.
